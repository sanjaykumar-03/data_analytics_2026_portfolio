# Day 2 Journal — Defining My Target

## Target Role

**Entry-Level Data Analyst**

My primary focus for the next 60 days is to become job-ready for an Entry-Level Data Analyst role. While I am interested in AI and Machine Learning, my immediate goal is to build strong skills in SQL, Excel, Power BI, Python, and data analysis so I can secure a good placement and gain industry experience.

---

## 3 Target Companies

1. **Zoho** — Why: Strong data-driven culture, excellent learning opportunities, and roles that value SQL, analytics, and problem-solving.

2. **KGISL** — Why: Good opportunities for fresh graduates in data-related roles and a chance to gain practical experience in analytics.

3. **Cognizant (CTS)** — Why: Offers analyst and technology roles with structured training, career growth, and exposure to real-world business data.

---

## My 60-Day Goal

By the end of 60 days, I will:

* Complete at least **5 end-to-end data analytics projects**.
* Solve **60 SQL interview questions**.
* Build a professional GitHub portfolio showcasing my projects and SQL practice.
* Be confident with SQL, Excel, Power BI, Python (pandas), and basic statistics.
* Be ready to apply for entry-level Data Analyst positions and perform well in technical interviews.

---

## Why This Matters to Me

I want to build a successful career in data analytics and become financially independent. Learning these skills will help me secure a good placement after my degree and create opportunities for long-term career growth. Even when learning feels difficult, I want to remember that every small step brings me closer to the career I want.
